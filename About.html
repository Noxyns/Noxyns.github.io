About Us

We are Passionate about Hair

Nox Hair Product is a natural hair brand, created for the under served needs of women of color. We are passionate about both Hair & Health. We developed & locally manufacture a natural-based hair care system specifically formulated for the needs of Afro, Kinky, Ethnic type hair. This hair is very fragile & dry by nature compared to other hairtypes and achieving optimal moisture & hairgrowth has always a challenge..until now.
